Directives

There are three types of directives:

  • A component is a directive.
  • Structural-Directives add, remove, or replace elements in the DOM.
    *ngIf and *ngFor are the most common examples.
  • Attribute-Directives change the appearance, or behaviour of elements.
    [ngClass] and [(ngModel)] are the most common examples.
With *ngIf, an element gets rendered or not based on a condition.

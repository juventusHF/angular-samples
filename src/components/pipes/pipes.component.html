Pipes

Pipes are used to transform data for display.
There are many predefined pipes in Angular, such as:

  • date: {{today | date}}
  • uppercase: {{'some text' | uppercase}}
  • lowercase: {{'Some Text' | lowercase}}
  • currency: {{amount | currency: 'CHF'}}
  • percent: {{percent | percent}}
  • number: {{pi | number:'1.1-2'}}
  • ...
  • pipes can be chained: {{today | date: 'fullDate' | uppercase}}

Custom pipes can be defined as well (see exponential-strength.pipe.ts)

2^10 = {{2 | exponentialStrength:10}}
